About Us

Lance Polly Holdings is a dynamic and diversified conglomerate operating across high-growth sectors in Kenya and beyond — including energy, real estate, hospitality, logistics, automotive services, imports, and exports.

Our Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To empower communities and drive progress through essential services and quality products.
  • Vision: To be East Africa’s most trusted diversified brand across industries.
  • Legal Structure: Private holding company with strategically decentralized subsidiaries.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our dealings.
  • Innovation: We embrace change and continuously seek better solutions.
  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering quality in every service we provide.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize environmental and social responsibility in our operations.

Our Founders

Mrs. Loreen Kasidi and Mrs. Modesta Meela are the visionary co-founders of Lance Polly Holdings. Their mission began in 2030 with a dream to transform industries through bold leadership and reliable services.

Starting with fuel distribution and real estate development, they strategically grew Lance Polly into a multi-sectoral powerhouse. Their deep understanding of business, government processes, and customer needs has built a company grounded in trust, sustainability, and impact.

Under their leadership, Lance Polly Holdings has expanded into energy, hospitality, logistics, automotive services, and more — always with a focus on empowering communities and driving progress.
